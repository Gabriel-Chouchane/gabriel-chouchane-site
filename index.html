I'm a certified intercultural communications expert, fluent in five languages (English, French, Arabic, German, and Spanish), and I've built three companies from the ground up. Whether you need a speaker for your next event or someone to build and run a podcast that gives your company a voice, I bring the expertise, the network, and the on-camera presence to make it happen.
